How it works

Señal alerts your friends if you can't.

Text Señal if you are ever concerned for your safety. Señal will check-in with you in 10 minutes. Text back to let Señal know you're OK, otherwise, we'll alert your 2-5 contacts.
To sign up, text a blank message to 999-999-9999.

Normal text messaging rates apply.
Cancel anytime.
